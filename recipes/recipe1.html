Chicken Sandwhich

Description

A creamy, savory sandwich made with shredded chicken mixed in a light mayo-based dressing. It’s simple, filling, and great for meal prep.

Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken breast (shredded or diced)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery (finely chopped)
  • Onion (optional, finely chopped)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bread (white, whole wheat, or toasted)
  • Lettuce (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, mayonnaise, celery, and onion.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Mix until evenly coated and creamy.
  4. Spread the mixture onto one slice of bread.
  5. Add lettuce if desired, then top with another slice of bread.
  6. Slice and serve.
