Tuna Sandwhich

Description

A simple and popular sandwich made with canned tuna and mayonnaise, often enjoyed as a quick, protein-rich meal.

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna (drained)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Bread
  • Cucumber slices (optional)

Steps

  1. Drain the tuna and place it in a bowl.
  2. Add mayonnaise and mix well.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and a splash of lemon juice if using.
  4. Spread the tuna mixture onto bread.
  5. Add cucumber slices if desired.
  6. Close the sandwich, slice, and serve.
